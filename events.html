Holy Matrimony

Venue Salem Mar Thoma Church
Time 2:30 PM

In the Mar Thoma tradition, the groom will place the Manthrakodi, a sacred silk, over the bride's head, marking her formal entry into his family. He will then tie the Minnu around her neck: a gold pendant bearing a cross, strung on seven threads, each one a symbol of the sacred bond they are entering.

Come and be part of this moment!
