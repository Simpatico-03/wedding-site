Holy Matrimony

The Vibe — Kerala Heirloom

Rich silks, soft florals, the warmth of a monsoon afternoon. Dress like you're attending something beautiful because you are. Sarees, lehengas, sherwanis, kurtas — all wonderful. And if you'd like to really lean into the Kerala spirit, a mundu (for the men) or a mundum neriyathum (the traditional two-piece for women) would be absolutely at home here.
