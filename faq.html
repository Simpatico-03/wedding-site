Travel & Accommodation

The Holy Matrimony is at Salem Mar Thoma Church in Pandalam, Kerala. The Dinner Reception is at Eden Garden, also in the Pandalam area. Both venues are located in God's Own Country! You'll find directions and Google Maps links on our Events page.

The nearest major airport is Kottayam Airport (approximately 30 km away). For more options, you can fly into Cochin International Airport (Kochi), which is about 60 km from Pandalam. From there, you can take a car or taxi to the venue.

Pandalam has several good accommodation options ranging from budget to luxury. We recommend checking our Travel page for recommended hotels and resorts. Many guests also choose to stay in nearby Kottayam, which offers more options and is only 20 minutes away.
