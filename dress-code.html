Colour & Style Guide

Dress Code

Burgundy

Burgundy

Do Wear: Deep reds, maroons, wine shades. Pairs beautifully with gold accents.

Don't Wear: Bright reds, neon, or overly casual cuts.

Blush

Blush

Do Wear: Soft pinks, rose gold, champagne. Elegant and romantic.

Don't Wear: Hot pink, fluorescent shades.

Forest

Forest Green

Do Wear: Deep greens, emerald, sage. Kerala-inspired natural tones.

Don't Wear: Bright neon greens, lime shades.

Ivory

Ivory

Do Wear: Cream, off-white, champagne. Timeless and elegant.

Don't Wear: Pure white (reserved for bride), heavy fabrics.

Gold

Gold

Do Wear: Metallic gold, warm neutrals with gold accents. Celebratory!

Don't Wear: Silver or cool metallics.

General Guidelines

Formal Events

Holy Matrimony & Dinner Reception call for elegant, well-fitted formal wear. Think cocktail dresses, sarees, sherwanis, and suits.

Fabrics

Silk, satin, linen, and cotton blends. Kerala's tropical climate calls for breathable, quality fabrics.

Accessories

Gold jewelry preferred. Minimal, elegant pieces that complement your outfit without overwhelming.
