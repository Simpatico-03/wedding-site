Holy Matrimony

Date Thursday, August 20, 2026
Time 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Venue Salem Mar Thoma Church YMCA Road, Thumpamon, Pandalam, Kerala 689502

In the Mar Thoma tradition, marriage is not just a ceremony. It is a covenant. A promise made before God, witnessed by the people who love you most.

At the heart of our service is the exchange of vows, words spoken not just to each other, but offered upward. The groom will place the Manthrakodi, a sacred silk, over the bride's head, marking her formal entry into his family. He will then tie the Minnu around her neck: a gold pendant bearing a cross, strung on seven threads, each one a symbol of the sacred bond they are entering.

Come and be part of this moment. Come and pray over us.
